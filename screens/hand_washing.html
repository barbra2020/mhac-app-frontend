Write about how to wash hands so that we kill the virus

ways to wash hands

  1. 1.Clean your hands regularly
  2. 2.Wash your hands with soap and water and dry them thoroughly
  3. 3.Use alcohol-based handrub if you dont have immediate access to soap and water

When to wash hands

  1. 1.Before,during or after preparing food
  2. 2.Before and after eating food
  3. 3.Before and after caring for the sick
  4. 4.Before and after treating a cut or a wound
  5. 5.After using the toilet
  6. 6.After blowing nose,coughing or sneezing
  7. 7.After touching an animal, animal feed, or animal waste
  8. 8.After touching garbage

Steps to wash your hands

  1. 1.Wet your hands with clean, running water, tap off the tap and apply soap
  2. 2.Lather your hands by rubbing them together with soap
  3. 3.Scrub your hands for atleast 20 seconds
  4. 4.Rinse your hands well under clean, running water
  5. 5.Dry your hands using a clean towel or air dry them

Hand washing process

Step 1, wet your hands with water

Step 2, Apply enough soap to cover all hand surfaces

Step 3, Rub hands palm to palm

Step 4, Right palm over left dorsum with interlaced fingers and vice versa

Step 5, Palm to palm with fingers interlaced

Step 6, Backs of fingers to opposing palms with fingers interlocked

Step 7, Rotational rubbing of left thumb clasped in right palm and vice versa

Step 8, Rotational rubbing backwards and forwards with clasped fingers of right hand in left palm and vice versa

Step 9, Rinse hands with water

Step 10, Dry thoroughly with a single use towel

Step 11, Use towel to turn off tap

Step 12, And your hands are safe
